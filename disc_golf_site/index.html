The Gear

Carry at least a putter, a midrange, and a driver. Stability (turn/fade) and plastic affect flight and grip.

  • Comfortable putter for confidence
  • Neutral midrange to learn angles
  • Control driver before distance

Course Strategy

Play for position. On tight fairways, a safe placement often beats risky distance. Learn to scramble smart.

Hole TypePlan
Open (Windy)Stable discs, lower lines
WoodedNeutral disc, smooth release
Uphill/DownhillAdjust power & nose angle

Etiquette

Let faster groups play through, mind your foot faults, and celebrate others’ great shots. Respect the course.
