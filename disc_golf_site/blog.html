Tips & Resources

Technique checklists, form cues, and links for further learning.

Backhand Fundamentals

  • Stand tall, relaxed grip—build from a one-step
  • Reach-back on a straight line; keep the disc close to chest
  • Shift from behind: plant foot braces, hips lead the shoulders
  • Finish balanced; don’t over-rotate your head off target

Forehand (Sidearm) Keys

  • Palm to the sky on follow-through to avoid excessive roll
  • Use stable/overstable discs while learning
  • Short, snappy motion—let the wrist do work
