About Disc Golf

Disc golf is played like traditional golf, but with flying discs and metal baskets. Each hole begins at a tee and ends when your disc rests in the basket or chains.

How to Play

  • Tee off from the designated pad
  • Subsequent throws from where the disc landed
  • Least throws to finish wins

Scoring quick guide: eagle (−2), birdie (−1), par (0), bogey (+1).

Discs & Stability

Putter

Slow, straight, forgiving—great for approaches and the chains.

Midrange

Versatile for controlled shots; learn angles and nose control here.

Driver

Fast but unforgiving. Start with control drivers before max-distance molds.
